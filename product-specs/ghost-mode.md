# Product Spec: Ghost Mode

> *The financial autopilot that makes ₹0-effort wealth creation real*

## The Premise

What if you could completely stop thinking about money and end up wealthier than if you'd spent 10 hours a month managing it?

Ghost Mode is the ultimate expression of Level 3 autonomy. You turn it on. You forget about it. Your money manages itself. You get a monthly "health check" like a blood report — glance at it, everything's green, move on with your life.

The name "Ghost" is deliberate. Your financial management becomes invisible. A ghost in the machine, silently optimizing every rupee.

## How It Works

### Activation

1. Connect Account Aggregator (one consent flow — 2 minutes)
2. Answer 7 questions about your life (not "risk tolerance" — actual life questions):
   - What matters most to you financially? (family security / early retirement / building something / travel and experiences / flexibility)
   - What's the biggest financial fear you have?
   - Do you have any major life changes coming in the next 2-3 years?
   - If you lost your job tomorrow, how many months could you survive?
   - Do you own a home or plan to buy one?
   - Who depends on you financially?
   - How old do you want to be when you stop working?
3. Ghost Mode analyzes your *complete* financial picture via AA: income, spending, investments, insurance, loans, tax filings
4. It presents "The Diagnosis" — a brutally honest, plain-language assessment of where you stand
5. You approve "The Plan" — a personalized, continuously-optimized financial strategy
6. You turn on Ghost Mode

That's it. You're done. Go live your life.

### What Ghost Mode Does (Daily)

**Cash Flow Optimization**
- Monitors all bank accounts in real-time via AA
- Sweeps idle cash above a smart threshold into overnight/liquid funds (earning 6-7% vs. 3.5% in savings)
- Pre-positions cash for upcoming debits (rent, EMIs, SIPs, insurance premiums) to avoid failed transactions
- Identifies subscription leaks and alerts you: "You're paying for Apple Music (₹99/mo), Spotify (₹119/mo), and YouTube Premium (₹129/mo). You used Spotify for 3 hours last month and the others not at all."

**Investment Management**
- Runs SIPs on optimal dates (not the 1st — when NAV is typically higher — but statistically optimal dates based on historical patterns)
- Rebalances across asset classes when allocation drifts beyond 5% from target
- Switches underperforming funds (12-month rolling alpha below threshold) to better alternatives
- Tax-loss harvests: identifies short-term capital loss opportunities daily, executes to offset gains
- Step-up SIPs: automatically increases SIP amounts when income increases (detected via salary credit patterns)

**Protection Management**
- Monitors insurance coverage against actual needs (computed from dependents, lifestyle, income)
- Alerts 60 days before renewals with comparison analysis
- Auto-renews optimal policies (with user's standing approval)
- Files insurance claims when it detects relevant transactions (hospital bills, accident repairs)

**Tax Optimization**
- Computes real-time tax liability using actual income and deduction data
- Optimizes between old and new regime *every month* (not once a year)
- Schedules advance tax payments on the last possible optimal date
- Ensures Section 80C, 80D, 80G, and HRA claims are maximized
- Alerts when a specific action could save tax: "If you invest ₹12,000 in NPS before March 31, you'll save ₹3,720 in tax."

**Emergency Response**
- Detects unusual large debits (medical, accident, legal)
- Activates emergency protocol: assesses insurance coverage → identifies cheapest capital source → executes → plans recovery
- Maintains emergency runway at user's approved level (default: 6 months)

## The Business Model

**Pricing**: ₹199/month (₹1,999/year) for the base tier. ₹499/month for premium (includes tax filing, insurance claims management, and priority support).

**Why this works**: At ₹199/month, Ghost Mode needs to save the user more than ₹199/month to justify itself. Based on modeling:
- Cash sweep optimization alone saves ₹200-500/month (for ₹5L+ in savings accounts)
- Tax-loss harvesting saves ₹500-2,000/month for active investors
- Tax regime optimization saves ₹2,000-6,000/month for salaried professionals
- Insurance comparison saves ₹500-2,000/month (amortized annual savings)

Total typical savings: ₹3,000-10,000/month. The product pays for itself 15-50x over.

**No commissions. Ever.** Ghost Mode recommends direct plans, cheapest insurance, optimal products — because its incentive is to maximize YOUR outcomes, not product revenue.

## The Wedge

Ghost Mode is too ambitious to launch as a complete system. The wedge:

**Phase 1**: Ghost Mode for Cash — just the cash sweep optimization. Connect AA, automatically sweep idle cash. Tangible, measurable, low-risk value from day one. ₹0 pricing.

**Phase 2**: Ghost Mode for Tax — add continuous tax optimization. The user sees real tax savings. Trust builds. ₹99/month.

**Phase 3**: Ghost Mode for Investments — add investment management, rebalancing, tax-loss harvesting. ₹199/month.

**Phase 4**: Full Ghost Mode — add insurance, emergency response, goal tracking. ₹499/month.

Each phase builds trust for the next. By Phase 4, you've been silently managing someone's cash for 6-12 months. They *trust* you. The autonomy boundary expands naturally.
