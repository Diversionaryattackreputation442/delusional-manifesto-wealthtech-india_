Data Topology

Five Tiers of Financial Awareness

T1

AA Native

Standardized. Consent-based. Live. Bank accounts, mutual funds, NPS, FDs — direct via Account Aggregator protocol.

T2

AA Emerging

Insurance, demat (CDSL/NSDL), tax (26AS/AIS), credit cards — standards exist, FIP adoption underway.

T3

Platform APIs

Zerodha Kite, Google Fit, Apple Health, Google Calendar — direct APIs, per-platform integration required.

T4

Transaction Inference

Where 80% of the magic lives. Parse UPI/bank transaction descriptions to detect Swiggy, Netflix, rent, salary, EMIs — no additional consent needed.

T5

User-Volunteered

Goals, career, family, salary slips, Form 16 — what only the user can know. Collected via conversation and onboarding.

The Belief

Why this, why India, why now

"What if we built for the India of 2036, not the India of 2026 with a slightly better UX?"
97% of Indian households with zero equity exposure
80%+ of Indians with no term insurance or adequate health cover
₹50K avg annual savings left on the table per household — not negotiated

India has UPI, the Account Aggregator framework, DigiLocker, ONDC, and OCEN — the most advanced financial data infrastructure on earth. The intelligence required to make optimal financial decisions is now accessible, cheap, and effective. The wealthtech companies that win will not have the best app. They'll have the best autonomic nervous system.
